Here are a few tips for choosing a goal. Make sure it's SMART:

  1. Specific ("Run a 5K" is better than "Exercise more.")
  2. Measurable (something you can break down into specific steps)
  3. Achievable (reach for the stars, but remember how good it feels to check something off your list)
  4. Results-Focused (think about what you want to have done)
  5. Time-bound (set a completion date and keep in mind how much you'll need to do each day/week)

The title of your goal should complete the sentence "I want to ____."
